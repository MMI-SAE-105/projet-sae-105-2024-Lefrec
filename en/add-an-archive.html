Rules to Follow

Do not use hateful or intentionally offensive language, nor racism, sexism, personal attacks, or defamation against individuals or organizations.

Do not use stolen or copied content.

Respect the site's spirit and have fun.

You are allowed to invent and imagine future events.

You are encouraged to use a pseudonym for the author and invent a publication date consistent with your archive's content.

If no existing category fits your archive, you can suggest adding a new category to the ArchEVE team.
