Archives of the future

In the world ahead, humanity has changed significantly and no longer resembles what you are today. Through technology, medicine, or ambition, the human of the future will be a transhuman.

But the origin of this transhumanist era is already in your hands and future archaeologists are studying it carefully.

Explore the archives to discover who or what changed the face of humanity by being the genesis of transhumanism, and most importantly, how.

Contribute to the archives

The ArchEVE project is above all a collaborative effort. Join future archaeologists by adding your own archive. Share your vision of the world and the future through the study of a film, book, game, etc.

By writing your own archives, you help build a collective memory that will guide future archaeologists in their quest for understanding. Each text is a unique piece added to this mosaic, a valuable testimony on the evolution of our time and the visions shaping the world to come.
